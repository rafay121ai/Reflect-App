Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

Reflect ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

What We Collect

Account information: Your email address when you create an account.

Reflection content: The thoughts, answers, and reflections you write inside the app. This content is private to you and never shared publicly.

Mood data: Optional mood check-ins you complete after reflections.

Usage data: Basic information about how you use the app (e.g. reflection frequency) used only to generate your personal insights.

How We Use Your Data

Your reflection content is sent to OpenAI's API to generate personalized responses. OpenAI processes this data according to their privacy policy. We do not use your content to train AI models.

We do not sell your data. We do not share your data with third parties except as required to operate the service (Supabase for data storage, OpenAI for AI responses, Railway for hosting).

Data Storage

Your data is stored securely in Supabase (PostgreSQL database). All data is private per user — no one else can access your reflections.

Your Rights

You can delete your account and all associated data at any time from the Settings screen inside the app. Deletion is permanent and irreversible.

You can request a copy of your data by contacting us at support@reflectapp.com.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, all data is permanently deleted within 24 hours.

Children

Reflect is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy. We will notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notice.

Contact

For privacy questions: support@reflectapp.com
